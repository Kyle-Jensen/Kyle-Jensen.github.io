How We Started

Goals

KBK Hardware has contracted us (Brandon, Kevin, Kyle) to build them a website where consumers can select hardware devices for their PC to be built at KBK’s Warehouse. Additionally, the site will offer full pre-built PC builds for customers to order online! The site will allow consumers to order specific PC hardware products to customize their PC build or buy pre-built PC’s.

Audience

PC users, Gamers, IT professionals, & Computer enthusiasts! User enters into website homepage (KBKHardware.com) and presses the shop top at the top. Next, the user presses the search bar to search for a hardware product or selects the category button to view the multiple PC hardware categories to find the item they are looking for. Furthermore, the customer selects the item(s) they are needing for their PC build and then continues to checkout!

Road Map

Our first goal of the project is to think of achievable ideas and plans that we will be able to achieve with our current knowledge with web development The next step would be to find a suitable template that will suit our site. Since our current plan is more on the retail side of things, we will then look for sites that cater towards selling merchandise. By utilizing an interactive and responsive website, we will be able to demonstrate a quality service that shows and provides various hardware devices that users can purchase or inspect. We are currently brainstorming more deliverables, however, at this time we still want to make the scope of the project realistic. One idea we might add will be a F.A.Q.
